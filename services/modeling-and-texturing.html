Modeling and Texturing

3D Modeling & Texturing Services

Create stunning digital assets with our professional 3D modeling and texturing services. We specialize in crafting highly detailed and optimized 3D models with photorealistic textures for any application.

What We Offer:

  • Organic 3D Modeling (Characters, Creatures)
  • Hard Surface Modeling (Products, Architecture, Vehicles)
  • UV Mapping and Unwrapping
  • Photorealistic PBR Material Creation
  • High-Poly to Low-Poly Optimization
  • Texture Painting & Substance Designer Workflows
  • Normal, Displacement & Bump Mapping
  • Scan-Based Texture Creation

Our modeling and texturing team combines technical precision with artistic flair to create assets that meet the highest industry standards. Whether you need characters for animation, products for visualization, or assets for games and virtual environments, we deliver models that are not only beautiful but also optimized for your specific needs.

We work with a variety of software including Blender, Maya, ZBrush, Substance Designer/Painter, and more to ensure your assets are created with the most appropriate tools for the job.
